About Our Company

Your Trusted Partner in Industrial Automation

AS Automation Company Chennai is a leading supplier and service provider of industrial automation products and solutions. Established by Mr. Azarudeen and Mr. Selva Kumar, we have built a strong reputation for delivering quality automation components and expert technical services across Tamil Nadu and South India.

We are authorized dealers and distributors for world-renowned brands including Delta, Siemens, Schneider Electric, Mitsubishi Electric, Omron, Mean Well, and Trinity Touch. Our comprehensive product portfolio covers everything from basic sensors to advanced PLC systems and servo drives.
